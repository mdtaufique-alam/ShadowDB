Project Codename: Night Falcon

In March 2025 a research team developed a new experimental battery technology called Helios Cells.

The Helios battery research project was led by Dr. Arjun Mehta, with support from engineer Lina Zhao and data scientist Rafael Costa.

Helios Cells are designed to power laptops for up to 72 hours continuously.

The design avoids lithium and instead uses sodium-ion chemistry combined with graphene membranes.

The entire research project cost approximately $2.3 million.
